My Education

Joining the college as a computer science undergrad I was first exposed to the world of economics in second semester after which taking up a minor degree in Finance in the fourth semester just came naturally. Here, I discovered FinTech. FinTech is all the financial services which we are currently using or wish to have at the ease of our phone click. The recent developments in technology keeps the capability to change the face of the financial sector and how markets work. Technology would basically remove the guess work from this sector. With advancements in machine learning, predictive behavioral modelling and data-driven results, the systems can be made smart enough to learn the surrounding and suggest a suitable solution, i.e. the use of artificial intelligence in financing decisions. The aim is to connect all- from business, to individuals, to groups and subconsciously make their investment decisions better and lot easier.
